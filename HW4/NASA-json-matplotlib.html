Asteroids and Comets

Have you ever wondered about the different orbital classes of the asteroids and comets that have come close to hitting earth? If so, check out this pie chart.

The data in the pie chart depicts the percentage of asteroids and comets of different orbital classes. These classes include: Apollo, Amor, Aten, Jupiter-family Comet, and Comet. Popular Baby Names and Ethnicity

Have you ever wondered about the most popular baby names in the US and how they break down ethnically? If so, check out this bar graph

The data in the bar graph depicts the number of male and female baby names for Hispanic, White Non Hispanic, Black Non Hispanic and Asian ethnicities. The data represents the most popular baby names in the United States according to a nation-wide survey.
