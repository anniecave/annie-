Just where are students eating at the Claremont Colleges?

The liberty granted by the consortium doesn't only apply to class registration across the colleges. The unique freedom of choice also extends to the dining options, allowing students the oppertunity to eat meals at the school of their choice.

The options

  1. Collins (CMC)
  2. Frary (Pomona)
  3. Frank (Pomona)
  4. Oldenborg (Pomona)
  5. Hoch-Shanahan (Harvey Mudd)
  6. McConnell (Pitzer)
  7. Malott (Scripps)

So which dining hall is the favorite?

Well, it depends who you ask.
