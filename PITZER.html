Pitzer's Dining Hall: McConnell

McConnell Dining Hall is located on Pitzer's South Campus, next to Keck II and close the basketball courts.

McConnell's Hours of Operation:

  • M-F: 7:30-10:00, 11:15-1:30, 5:00-7:30
  • Weekends: 10:30-12:30, 5:00-6:30

McConnell is another brunch favorite, always serving omlettes and a solid variety of hot options in addition to the classic granolas and fruits. The desert bar is also always stocked with pasteries for breakfast and other yummy options during lunch and dinner. The dining hall frequently serves stir-fry which is a crowd favorite and is also sought out for its smoothies during breakfast
